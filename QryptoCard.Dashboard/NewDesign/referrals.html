How the program works

Step 1
Share your link

Send your code or link to a friend. They get a head start when they sign up with it.

Step 2
They top up a card

When your friend funds their first Kash card with USDT, the reward unlocks for both of you.

Step 3
You both earn

You earn a 0.1% commission on every top-up they make — paid in USDT, for life.

Commissions are credited in USDT to your available balance. The standard 3% top-up fee still applies to your friend's top-ups.
